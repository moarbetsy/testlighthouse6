About Sarah Jenkins

Lead Attorney & Founder

With over 15 years of courtroom experience, Sarah Jenkins has established a reputation for tenacious advocacy and unshakeable ethics. Graduating near the top of her class from State University Law School, she founded Jenkins Law with a singular mission: to provide accessible, high-quality legal representation to her community.

Sarah believes that every client deserves to be heard. She approaches every case with a fresh perspective, combining detailed legal knowledge with a pragmatic approach to problem-solving.
